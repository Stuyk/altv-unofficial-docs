Timecycle for Lighting

Timecycle Name for Lighting

Timecycles, Vertex Colors, and Lights are key for making decent looking lighting between interior and exterior. It is important that your limbo portal has a similar Timecycle to the one you are using inside. However, they do not have to match.

The best way to test time cycles is through FiveM and streaming your resource that way and swapping between cycles. However, you can supposedly do this with other clients. FiveM is apparently what most modders use for testing lighting.

Recommended Timecycle Names

NodirectLight

FullAmbientmult_interior

INT_FullAmbientmult

INT_NoAmbientmult

NoAmbientmult_interior
